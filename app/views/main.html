Introducing Grupr

Making grouping easier.

What is Grupr

Grupr is a mobile application designed to make finding and creating study groups easier.

How does Grupr benift me?

Grupr benifits you by connecting you to other students in your classes. So you, the guy who sits behind you, and the girl in the front row can study together.

Where can I get Grupr

Grupr will be available on the app store in the near future.
